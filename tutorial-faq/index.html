Tutorial

Hello and welcome to the guidebook for IdleRPG Bot. This bot is mainly focused around an adventure-styled game. To get started, you will first need to make a character. Type $create to do so.
After that, it will ask you to pick a name. Type a name. You do not need to include the prefix; if you do, it will be part of your name. For example:
$create
$Zell
The above will name my character $Zell so you do not need to include the prefix.
If you mess up the naming of your character, you can do $rename for no cost.

After that, you will start with $100 and some starter equipment (3 Atk/Def), so you will be pretty limited on what you can do.
Type $adventures to list all the adventures, and you will probably want to start with the first one, so type $adventure 1 (Or $a 1 to shorten it), and your character will embark on the adventure.
You can type $status (Or $s) to check on the status; you will need this command to complete the adventure when finished.
Every adventure takes time, the harder the difficulty, the more time it takes. The first one takes 30 minutes. Once the adventure is finished, the bot will calculate your success chances and you will either succeed on the mission or fail. If you succeed, you will receive money, experience, and a random piece of equipment. If you fail, you get nothing. Just embark on another adventure!

There are many ways to make money, you earn some from the adventures, you can sell your unwanted equipment (type $inventory or $i to see your inventory, this lists all items, their IDs, stats, and market value), if you want to put an item for sale to other players, type $sell id price and the item will be placed on the market. If you want to sell the item instantly for its value, type $merch id and you will receive the value.
You can also gamble! You can flip a coin to win (or lose) money. The command is $flip heads/tails amount, and at the moment there is no limit on how much you can bet. The chances are 50/50 and if you win, you receive the amount you bet, if you lose, you lose it.
There is also a $bet command where the bot will guess a number between 1 and the maximum you define (has to be at least 2), and if you guessed that number, you win the amount you bet.
For example:
$bet 2 1 500 - Starts a bet. The bot will guess a number between 1 and 2, and if it is 1, you win $500.

You can also battle people for money. The command is $battle amount. If you want to battle a specific player, you can mention a user after the amount to battle for. The battle is handled by adding each combatant’s Atk, Def, and then adding a random number between 1-7 to each combatant, and whoever is higher, wins. If both are equal, it randomizes the winner. The winner receives the prize money.

FAQ

Q: How do I make a character?
A: Type $create to start the creation process.

Q: Help! My character’s name is messed up. What do I do?
A: Type $rename to assign a different name to your character.

Q: How do I play the game?
A: Type $adventures to see a list of adventures; type $adventure # to embark on one.

Q: How do I see my stats and inventory?
A: You can type $profile (or $p for short) to show your profile, and $inventory (or $i for short) to see your inventory. You can also type $profile @user to show a user’s profile. If the image isn’t loading for you, you can type $p2 to show your profile, or $p2 @user to show someone else’s profile.

Q: Is there a way to customize the profile images?
A: Yes, you can do $profile white to display the text in white or $profile #RRGGBB to display the text in a given hex color code. Works for other users profiles too.

Q: How do I equip the gear I find on adventures?
A: Type $inventory to display them, and type $equip id to equip them.

Q: Is there a way to get free money?
A: You can do $daily to receive $50 per day. Money is also earned through adventures, gambling, selling equipment, or becoming a patron.

Q: How do I battle my friend?
A: Type $battle amount @friend, and your friend types private join @you, no bot prefix.

Q: How do I get my crate for upvoting the bot?
A: Type $claim.

Q: $claim is telling me to wait a lot of seconds, but I didn’t receive my crate, what do I do?
A: Join the IdleRPG Support Server, and drop a message in the #gimme-my-crate channel. In the future, use $vote to generate the link to upvote the bot. To join the support server, type $support.

Q: How do I see the leaderboards?
A: Type $richest for top 10 richest players, $highscore for top 10 leveled players, and $pvp for top 10 PvPers.

Q: How do I use the market?
A: You type $shop to open the shop, and the bot will add reactions at the bottom to navigate the market. Click on one to navigate it. You can click on the 1234 reaction to go to a specific page (based on your input). You then type $buy id to buy an item.

Q: Can I trade items without the shop?
A: You can type $offer id price @user to offer an item at a certain price to a player.

Q: WTF are Item IDs?
A: The item is referenced by an ID since names are randomly generated and it is highly likely you will end up with two items of the same name. This is how they are referenced when handling them through trades or marketing.

Q: I have a question that’s not listed here, what should I do?
A: Join the IdleRPG Support Server by typing $support and leave a message in #help and someone will be there to answer your question.
